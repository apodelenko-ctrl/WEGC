Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how WEST EAST TRADE GROUP PTE. LTD. (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares personal data when you visit our website or contact us about properties and related services.

We may share data with service providers (hosting, email, form processing) and, with your instruction, with developers for the specific project(s) you request. We do not sell your personal data.

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own. We use reasonable safeguards (contractual, technical, organizational) to protect it.

We keep data as long as necessary for the purposes described, or as required by law.

7) Your rights

Subject to law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability. To exercise these rights, email post@wegc.fund.

8) Children

9) Contact
