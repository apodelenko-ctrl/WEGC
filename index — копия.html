Ownership in Thailand: Leasehold vs Freehold

Short primer for international buyers. We’ll advise case by case.

Freeholdfull ownership
  • Full title to the unit + proportionate land share (condo quota up to 49% for foreigners).
  • Registered at Land Office; transfer taxes/fees apply.
  • Preferred for long-term holding and resale liquidity.
Leaseholdup to 30+30+30 yrs
  • Long-term lease structure; lower entry complexity and often faster transfers.
  • Unit rights per contract; common for foreign buyers in resort markets.
  • Attractive for usage + rental, depending on developer/operator program.

This is general information, not legal advice. We connect you with local counsel and manage the process end to end.
